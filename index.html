Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a simple and fun two-player game that uses a 3x3 grid. Players take turns marking the squares with either an "X" or an "O". The objective is to be the first to get three of your marks in a row, either across, down, or diagonally. If all nine squares are filled and no player has achieved this, the game ends in a draw.
